Give your furniture a 2nd home

Give your furniture a second chance at life! Generous Furniture Donors like you can help a family in need who cannot afford the furniture, while also keeping it from rotting in a landfill where no one can use it. Help us help others with your donations!

How we help

Sovereignty Abound donates many of the donated furniture directly to those in need. We also sell some of the furniture you donate in our thrift store, and we use the proceeds from the sales to help with the needs of the community.


How you can help

Decluttering your home? Change the life of a family in need by donating your older furniture!
